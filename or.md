Operations Research (OR) is a crucial subject in Industrial Engineering, focusing on applying mathematical models, statistical analysis, and optimization techniques to solve complex decision-making problems. The subject is divided into two main parts: OR1, which deals with foundational concepts such as linear programming, the simplex method, and basic optimization and OR2, which expands into advanced topics like integer programming, network optimization, and dynamic programming. These techniques help improve efficiency, reduce costs, and optimize performance in various industries.
