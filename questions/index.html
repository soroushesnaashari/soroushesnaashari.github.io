Questions

This section is for thought-provoking questions I'm excited to dig into. If you've got any answers, ideas or helpful resources, I'd love to hear from you, just shoot me an email:
soroushesnaashari@gmail.com

  1. Why can't humans teach other creatures how to build a civilization?
  2. If consciousness comes from certain brain processes, could a very complex computer ever really “feel” like we do, or is there always a gap between computer signals and human feelings?
  3. If I could make a perfect copy of my mind (every thought and memory), would that copy really be “me”, or is there something special about the original me that cannot be copied?
  4. If thought arises from physical processes, could a mind built on entirely different physics ever share our inner experience?
  5. If language shapes how we think, might there be parts of reality we cannot understand until we invent, or develop, a new way to talk about them?
