Scaling is an important step in preparing data for machine learning, which ensures that all features contribute equally to the performance of the model. In my previous article, Data Scaling 101: StandardScaler vs MinMaxScaler, we have seen why scaling matters and compared two popular methods. But is scaling really just about StandardScaler and MinMaxScaler?
No! Though these two are commonly used, they may not be the best for your dataset.
In this article, we will go beyond the basics and explore other powerful scaling methods like RobustScaler and MaxAbsScaler that will help us better handle complex datasets for more reliable model performance. Let’s dive deeper into the world of scaling!
