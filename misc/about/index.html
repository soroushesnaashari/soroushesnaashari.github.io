About

I’m Soroush, an Industrial Engineer with a solid foundation in mathematics, management, analytical thinking and problem solving. I studied at the Islamic Azad University, Central Tehran Branch. My academic journey culminated in a thesis on AI and its applications in industrial engineering, where I had the chance to explore various topics under the supervision of Prof. Parisa Shahnazari-Shahrezaei. That project deepened my passion for Artificial Intelligence and showed me how AI and its tools can transform traditional engineering practices.

During the last two years of university I dove into programming and the fundamentals of AI, and I started doing hands-on projects to put theory into practice. After graduation I kept learning, tackling more complex concepts (such as Deep Learning and Neural Networks) and building real systems.

Today I work in industry as a Data Crawler and Web Scraper, a role that’s given me practical experience with data pipelines and large-scale information collection and pushed me further toward the fields I want to grow in: Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

As I mentioned I’m really passionate about AI and I love transforming messy data into useful products and insights. My Industrial Engineering background gives me an engineering mindset and a set of practical soft skills, systems thinking, process optimization, project management and clear cross-team communication, that shape how I design and deliver AI solutions. Those skills help me keep models useful, scalable and aligned with real-world needs.

With experience across project management, data science, machine learning, applied analysis and web-scale data collection, I enjoy tackling hard problems and shipping solutions that actually help people and teams.

I look forward to continue my journey as energetic as possible. Let’s dive into the endless world of learning!
